# Output Format Specification

Based on analysis of official Flow Church Instagram Reels (@flow_thereispowerhere).

## Video Format

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Aspect ratio | 9:16 (1080x1920) |
| Codec | H.264 |
| Duration | 30 seconds to 2 minutes |
| Audio | Original sermon audio only (no music overlay) |
| Frame rate | 30fps |

## Visual Layout

### Speaker Framing
- Source video is wide-angle 16:9 stage shot
- **Crop and zoom** to center on Bishop Dag Heward-Mills
- Frame from **mid-chest up** — tight enough to see facial expressions
- AI face tracking to keep speaker centered as they move
- Dark vignette/gradient at bottom of frame to create contrast for captions

### Captions (Critical)
- **Style:** Word-by-word animated (one word highlighted at a time)
- **Font:** Bold, sans-serif, large (readable on mobile)
- **Color:** White text for all words
- **Highlight:** Current spoken word is highlighted in **yellow/gold OR red** color
- **Position:** Lower-center of video frame (above Instagram's native UI safe zone)
- **Background:** Subtle dark gradient behind text for readability
- **Timing:** Synced to audio using WhisperX word-level timestamps
- **Max characters per line:** ~25-30 characters
- **Lines on screen:** 1-2 at a time

### Branding
- FLOW Church logo in bottom-right corner (semi-transparent)
- Optional: Title card at start or end

## Content Structure

### Source Segment: "The Prophetic Blessing"
- Located after communion in FLOW Prayer Meeting services
- **Keyword triggers:** "the Lord bless you", "lift your hands", "receive this blessing"
- Typical duration: 5-8 minutes in full form
- Clip the most impactful 30s-2min portions

### Title/Description Format
- **Hook-style title:** Emotional/inspirational hook + "|" + "Prophetic Blessing" or "FLOW Prayer"
- Examples from official channel:
  - "If You're Facing a Storm, Listen to This Prayer | Prophetic Blessing | Dag Heward-Mills"
  - "God Says It's Not Over! | Prophetic Blessing | FLOW Prayer Meeting"
  - "PRAYERS CONCERNING THE FUTURE (FLOW PRAYER, 22ND AUG 2025)"

### Caption Text (Post Description)
- Inspirational one-liner related to the clip content
- Examples: "You are rising!", "The Holy Spirit is influencing your life as you receive"

## Platform-Specific Notes

### Instagram Reels
- Max 90 seconds for optimal algorithm boost
- Captions are essential (85%+ watch on mute)
- First 3 seconds must hook the viewer
- Use relevant hashtags: #FlowChurch #DagHewardMills #PropheticBlessing #Prayer

### TikTok
- Can go up to 3 minutes but 30-60s performs best
- Same vertical format
- TikTok algorithm favors watch-time completion (shorter = higher completion rate)
